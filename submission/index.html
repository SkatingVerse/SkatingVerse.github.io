The 1st SkatingVerse Workshop & Challenge


Morning, May 28th, lstanbul, Turkey

Easychair submissions Website

Paper should be submitted to EasyChair for review.

Authors are invited to submit long and short papers in the IEEE format. Submissions to 1st SkatingVerse Challenge should have no substantial overlap with any other paper submitted to FG 2024 or any paper already published. Papers presented at 1st SkatingVerse Challenge will appear in the IEEE Xplore digital library. Papers must follow the FG paper format (double-blind) .

We expect 30 paper submissions, and finally accept 2 oral papers and 8 poster papers, so the acceptance rate is about 30% (Oral paper will have an oral talk during conference).

SkatingVerse Submission Policies

Paper Submission Procedure: All paper are submitted via the email.

Paper Review Procedure: double-blind

Ethical considerations: The dataset was collected from the internet and the national Figure Skating Association in accordance with related regulations. To avoid the dataset being abused and ensure the privacy of the skaters, we will provide strict access for applicants who sign the license, and try our best to guarantee it for research purposes only.
